Amboss is a Lightning Network explorer and analytics platform — node rankings, channel maps, routing insights, and node performance history for anyone running a node. It's also home to Magma, a non-custodial marketplace where operators buy and sell channel liquidity directly from each other, priced by the market rather than a single provider.

For node runners, Amboss is as much an infrastructure tool as it is a directory: it's where you check how well-connected a potential peer actually is before opening a channel, and where you go when you need to source liquidity instead of waiting for it to arrive organically.
