Zaprite fills the gap between BTCPay Server and custodial payment processors. It's a SaaS invoicing tool that lets businesses issue professional invoices accepting Lightning and on-chain Bitcoin — all non-custodially. It connects directly to merchants' own Lightning nodes.

For freelancers and small businesses in the Bitcoin economy, Zaprite makes it easy to get paid in sats without the overhead of self-hosting a full BTCPay instance.
