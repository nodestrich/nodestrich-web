Breez is the only self-custodial Lightning wallet with an integrated merchant point-of-sale terminal. A phone becomes a full Lightning cash register with a toggle. Beyond the wallet, the Breez SDK lets any developer integrate Lightning payments into their app without running a full node.

For the Lightning ecosystem, Breez matters because every merchant using it is generating real payment volume that flows through the network — the kind of organic demand that makes running a routing node worthwhile.
