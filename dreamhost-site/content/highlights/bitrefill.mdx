Bitrefill is one of the most established Lightning commerce platforms, offering gift cards for thousands of brands including Amazon, Uber, and Netflix. Lightning payments confirm in seconds, and they offer a discount on all Lightning purchases.

It's the most practical bridge from Lightning to mainstream retail spending. For node operators, Bitrefill generates consistent real-world payment volume across the network and serves as a go-to answer for "what can I actually buy with Lightning?"
