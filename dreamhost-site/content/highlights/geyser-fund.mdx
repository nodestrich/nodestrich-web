Geyser Fund is the Lightning-native alternative to Kickstarter and GoFundMe. Creators launch projects and receive bitcoin directly via Lightning — no intermediary holds your funds. It also supports Nostr integration, so donations can be sent from any Nostr client.

Geyser has funded hundreds of Bitcoin community projects, from open-source development to real-world meetups. For node operators, it's a tangible example of Lightning enabling a new kind of permissionless fundraising that traditional payment rails can't match.
