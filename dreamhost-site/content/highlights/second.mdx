Second is a self-custodial wallet built on Ark, letting users send and receive Bitcoin instantly without opening or managing their own Lightning channels. Funds are held as vTXOs coordinated through an Ark Service Provider, with a pre-signed exit path back to Bitcoin L1 if the provider ever stops cooperating.

Second is one of the first production wallets built on Ark, making it a real-world example of channel-free Bitcoin payments — worth watching as an alternative onboarding path for users who want Lightning-like speed without the operational overhead of running a node.
